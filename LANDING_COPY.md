## 4. FAQ Section (5 Real Q&As)

### Q1: Do I need to connect my bank account or upload credit card statements to run this audit?
> **Answer:** Absolutely not. SpendSentry is fully zero-integration and self-reported. You simply select the tools your team uses, your current plans, and seats in our visual spend form. We process the audit instantly on-screen. No logins, no API keys, and no financial connections required.

### Q2: How does the "Cursor + Copilot" double-paying detection work?
> **Answer:** Many engineering organizations pay $19/user/month for GitHub Copilot Business while simultaneously paying $40/user/month for Cursor Business. Because Cursor includes state-of-the-art autocomplete (powered by custom models) and direct chat (supporting Claude 3.5 Sonnet and GPT-4o) natively, keeping a separate Copilot subscription for the same developer is completely redundant. SpendSentry flags this exact overlap and calculates your instant savings from canceling Copilot.

### Q3: How is my email and data handled? Is my audit public?
> **Answer:** Your privacy is our highest priority. The public shareable URL (`spend-sentry.com/share/[id]`) strips away all identifying details like your company name, email, or team member roles, displaying only the aggregate tool count and savings breakdown. Your raw data is stored securely in our private Postgres backend, and we will never sell or share your email address.

### Q4: How is Credex able to offer discounts on Claude, Cursor, and OpenAI?
> **Answer:** Startups pivot, resize, or overforecast their infrastructure budgets, leaving them with pre-paid, unused AI credits that they cannot refund. Credex buys these verified, surplus infrastructure credits at a deep discount and passes the savings directly to you. It's the exact same enterprise AI tools, run on the exact same official servers, but at up to 30% off retail pricing.

### Q5: What makes your audit recommendations "defensible"?
> **Answer:** We do not use speculative AI models to guess your billing numbers, nor do we push generic "downgrade everything" advice. All audit logic is powered by a rigid, deterministic financial engine using real-time pricing data directly from vendor pages (Cursor, Anthropic, OpenAI, GitHub). We calculate exact plan seat-minimums (e.g., Claude Team's 5-seat threshold) and API break-even points, delivering numbers that any CFO or finance manager can audit and verify.
